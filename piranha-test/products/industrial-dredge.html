  • Can pass up to 2.5” size solids

Features

Our Piranha industrial dredges feature powerful agitator slurry pumps that are so effective, they excavate material faster than other styles of dredges many times their size. Other manufacturer’s dredges skim materials off the top of your sediment layer, pumping excessive amounts of water with low solids content. Piranha dredges excavate from within the core of your material. Our dredges can be used for excavating gravel, sand, crushed ore, coal slurries, fly ash, wash fines, mud, and sewage & water treatment sludge. Our industrial dredges are customized and built to meet the specific needs of your application.
